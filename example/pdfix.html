PDFix turns messy PDFs into compliant, structured documents through powerful automated processing – saving time, reducing costs, and ensuring accessibility at scale. Their AI-driven technology effortlessly ensures adherence to WCAG, PDF/UA, and Section 508 standards while integrating smoothly with your existing document workflows.
